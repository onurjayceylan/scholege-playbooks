Page 1: Technical Setup

Equipment & Environment

Your teaching environment impacts student experience directly. Proper setup is essential for professional, effective classes.

Required Equipment:

  • Reliable computer (laptop or desktop)
  • High-quality microphone (USB headset recommended)
  • Webcam or built-in camera
  • Stable internet connection (5+ Mbps)
  • Neutral, professional background
Audio Quality Matters: Poor audio is more disruptive than poor video. Invest in a quality microphone.

Page 2: Internet Requirements

Connection Stability

Your internet connection is critical for uninterrupted classes.

Minimum Requirements:

  • Download: 5 Mbps minimum, 10+ Mbps recommended
  • Upload: 5 Mbps minimum, 10+ Mbps recommended
  • Ping: Under 100ms (lower is better)
  • Wired Ethernet connection preferred over WiFi
  • Dedicated bandwidth (avoid sharing with heavy users)

Test your connection at speedtest.net before your first class.

Page 3: Before Class Checklist

30-Minute Preparation

Arrive early and complete this checklist to ensure a smooth class experience.

Pre-Class Tasks (30 minutes before):

  • Check internet connection (speed test if needed)
  • Test microphone and camera
  • Close unnecessary applications
  • Restart router if connection seems weak
  • Review student profile and lesson plan
  • Prepare teaching materials and screen share
  • Ensure professional appearance
  • Test audio volume (not too loud/quiet)

Page 5: During Class Tips

Best Practices While Teaching

Maintain professional, effective communication during your classes.

During-Class Guidelines:

  • Start exactly on time
  • Use professional language and tone
  • Maintain good posture and eye contact with camera
  • Minimize background distractions
  • Stay patient and encouraging
  • Ask clarifying questions if student is confused
  • Use screen share to enhance learning
  • Give clear feedback and corrections

Page 6: Troubleshooting Common Issues

Quick Fixes for Technical Problems

Most technical issues have simple solutions. Try these before contacting support.

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Microphone Not Working: Check mute settings, try restarting app
  • Camera Not Showing:strong> Check device permissions, try another browser
  • Connection Dropping: Move closer to router, reduce background apps
  • Audio Delay: Close other applications, check internet speed
  • Screen Share Not Working:strong> Restart browser, check permissions
  • Student Can't See/Hear Me: Check your device settings, restart

Page 7: After Class Procedures

Post-Class Duties

Your responsibilities don't end when the class does. Complete these tasks promptly.

Post-Class Checklist:

  • Thank the student and confirm next class time
  • Write detailed progress notes within 2 hours
  • Mark class as completed in platform
  • Highlight improvements and areas for growth
  • Suggest homework or practice activities
  • Note any technical issues encountered
  • Respond to student questions in chat
  • Flag any concerns about student engagement
