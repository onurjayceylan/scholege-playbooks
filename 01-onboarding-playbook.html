Your First Week at Scholege

Complete Onboarding Playbook for New Teachers

Page 1: Welcome to Scholege!

Let's Get You Started

Welcome to the Scholege team! We're excited to have you join our community of passionate English teachers. This playbook will guide you through your first week and set you up for success.

Over the next 7 days, you'll:

  • Complete your profile setup
  • Understand our platform and teaching philosophy
  • Meet your support team
  • Prepare for your first classes
  • Learn about payment and invoicing
Pro Tip: Keep this playbook handy during your first week. We reference specific pages and resources you'll need.

Page 3: Setting Up Your Profile

Complete Your Teacher Profile

Your profile is your first impression on Scholege. Students browse teacher profiles before booking classes, so make yours compelling!

Required Information:

  • Professional photo (clear, smiling, professional attire)
  • Bio: 150-200 words about your teaching experience and style
  • Teaching background and qualifications
  • Specializations (e.g., Business English, Conversational, Test Prep)
  • Your timezone and availability
Quality Matters: Teachers with complete, professional profiles get booked 3x more often.

Page 4: First Class Preparation

Get Ready for Day One

Your first class is a chance to make a great impression and understand what teaching on Scholege is like.

Before Your First Class:

  • Review the student's profile and goals
  • Test your audio and video equipment
  • Have a reliable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
  • Prepare an icebreaker activity
  • Arrive 5 minutes early to test the platform
Technical Support: Contact Burak at burak@scholege.com for any platform issues

What We Provide

Scholege provides comprehensive teaching materials and resources to support your lessons.

Page 7: Performance Expectations

Setting Yourself Up for Success

Here's what we expect from our teachers and what students expect from you:

Key Expectations:

  • Professional behavior and communication at all times
  • Punctuality: Start classes exactly on time
  • Prepared lessons that match student goals
  • Feedback and progress notes after each class
  • Responsiveness to student and admin messages (within 24 hours)
Performance Reviews: Conducted monthly by Selinay (selinay@scholege.com)
