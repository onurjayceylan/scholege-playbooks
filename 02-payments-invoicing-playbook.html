Your rate depends on several factors including experience, certifications, student feedback, and specialization.

Page 3: Setting Up Payment Info

Bank Account & Payment Details

Before you can receive payments, you must complete your banking information in your Scholege account.

Required Information:

  • Full name as it appears on bank account
  • Bank account number
  • Routing number (US) or SWIFT/IBAN code (International)
  • Country of residence
  • Tax ID or SSN (if applicable)

Complete this in your Account Settings → Payment Information section.

Page 4: How Payments Work

The Payment Process

Understanding our payment cycle ensures you receive your money on time.

Monthly Payment Process:

  • Classes Completed: You teach classes throughout the month
  • Invoice Generation: On the 25th, system automatically generates your invoice
  • Invoice Review:strong> You review and confirm the invoice
  • Payment Processing: By the 5th of next month, payment transfers to your bank
  • Arrival Time: Typically 3-5 business days depending on bank

Page 5: Creating Invoices

Invoice Submission Guide

Invoices are generated automatically, but you need to review and confirm them.

Invoice Details Include:

  • Number of classes taught
  • Hourly rate and total earnings
  • Banking information for transfer
  • Invoice number for tracking
Invoice Issues? Contact Burçin immediately at burçin@scholege.com if you notice discrepancies

Page 6: Tax Considerations

Understanding Your Tax Obligations

As an independent contractor, you're responsible for your own taxes. Scholege provides information but not tax advice.

Important Points:

  • Keep all invoices and payment records for tax purposes
  • You may need to file quarterly estimated taxes
  • Request 1099 form if you're a US contractor
  • International teachers: Check local tax laws
  • Consider deducting business expenses
Not Tax Advice: Consult a tax professional for your specific situation.

Page 8: Best Practices

Ensuring Smooth Payments

Follow these practices to avoid payment delays or issues.

Payment Best Practices:

  • Mark all classes as completed immediately after teaching
  • Verify banking information is correct and current

  • Report discrepancies within 48 hours
  • Keep records of all classes and payments
  • Respond quickly to payment-related questions from Burçin
Quick Reference: Burçin (burçin@scholege.com) handles all payment matters and can resolve issues quickly.
